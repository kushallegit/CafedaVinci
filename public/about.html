About Cafe Da Vinci

Our goal is to create meaningful experiences for everyone we encounter. With this in mind, we've dedicated ourselves to a positive vision for the future—one that supports and nurtures both those within our organization and those outside of it.

Our company is built on the core values of harm reduction, child safety, and education. These principles guide every decision we make. Driven by the vision of achieving the greatest good, our mission is to foster a well-informed public for the betterment of our community and future generations.

Mission

We believe that responsible living involves not only personal growth but also making a positive impact locally. Our goal is to help build healthier communities and enhance the lives of those within them. Rooted in events and outreach, we seize every opportunity to give back to our community through local initiatives and a variety of educational resources. We aim to engage, inspire, and consistently foster a sense of fun.

Our Passion

We approach coffee with a sense of wonder, always eager to explore new methods, equipment, and insights. Driven by a healthy curiosity, we are constantly discovering new and exciting possibilities that push the boundaries of what coffee can be. This relentless pursuit of innovation not only enhances our craft but also deepens our connection to the world of coffee, making each discovery an exciting adventure.

Our Path to the 1st Anniversary

As we approach our first anniversary, we’re filled with gratitude. It’s been an incredible ride, and we’re proud of how far we’ve come. From our humble beginnings to now being a cherished local cafe, we are thankful for the unwavering support of our customers and community. The journey to this point hasn’t been easy, but it has been worth every moment. We’re excited for what’s next, and we can’t wait to continue sharing our passion for coffee with all of you.

Located in the heart of Toronto, we invite you to share our love and passion for coffee and community. We offer a cozy atmosphere with a wide variation in music and a mix of people from all cultures and ages. Be welcomed by friendly staff every time you visit, or Shop Online from the comfort of your home.
